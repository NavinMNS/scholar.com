About Us



SCHOLAR is India's largest ed-tech company and the creator of India's most loved school learning app. Launched in 2015, SCHLOAR offers highly personalised and effective learning programs for classes 1 - 12 (K-12), and aspirants of competitive exams like JEE, IAS etc. With 50 million registered students and 3.5 million paid subscriptions, SCHOLARS has become one of the most preferred education platforms across the globe.
